Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear when visiting the monasteries?
Please wear modest clothing that covers your shoulders and knees. Respectful attire is mandatory for entry into sacred prayer halls.
Are the retreats suitable for beginners?
Yes! Our experiences are designed for all levels. The monks provide gentle guidance for those new to meditation and Buddhist practices.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 7 days prior to the event are eligible for a full refund. 80% of your fee is still donated to the monastery regardless of attendance.
