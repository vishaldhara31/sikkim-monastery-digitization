Our Mission

Preserving the spiritual heritage of the Himalayas through digital storytelling and immersive technology.

Guardians of Shangri-La

Monastery360 was born in 2025 from a deep respect for the sacred traditions of Sikkim. We believe that technology should not replace tradition, but rather serve as a bridge to protect it for future generations.

By documenting the architecture, rituals, and daily lives within these ancient monasteries, we provide a window into a world of peace and profound wisdom.

Immersive Documentation

Using 360-degree photography to capture every intricate detail of sacred murals and statues.

Cultural Education

Providing context and history for the rituals that have defined the Himalayan spirit for centuries.

Sustainable Tourism

Encouraging respectful visits that support local monastic communities and ecology.

Experience the Silence

Discover the events that bring these mountains to life.
