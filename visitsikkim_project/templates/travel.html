Logistics & Permits

Entry Permits (ILP)

Foreign nationals require an Inner Line Permit (ILP) to enter Sikkim. Indian nationals require Protected Area Permits (PAP) for restricted areas like North Sikkim and Nathu La.

How to Reach

By Air: Pakyong Airport (Limited) or Bagdogra (IXB) which is 125km from Gangtok.

By Road: Taxis are available from Siliguri and NJP station. We recommend private SUVs for mountain roads.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March - May)

Ideal for the Flower Festival and Rhododendron treks. Clear skies and blooming valleys.

Autumn (Oct - Dec)

Perfect for mountain views and monastery festivals. Crisp air and golden landscapes.

Best Stays

Packing Essentials

  • Heavy woolens (even in Summer)
  • Comfortable hiking shoes
  • Personal medicine for altitude
  • Power bank & extra batteries
  • Respectful attire for temples
